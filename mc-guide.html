10:00

"Bismillahir-Rahmanir-Raheem.
As-salāmu ʿalaykum wa rahmatullāhi wa barakātuh.

Welcome to Symposium Group 2 of the TIM 3rd Ijtimāʿ, 1447 AH.

Today, we gather not simply to discuss technology — but to ask a deeper question: What kind of future are we building, and for whom are we building it?

Our theme this year — Beyond Personal Success: Raising Purposeful Professionals and Building a Model Ummah — calls each of us to look beyond ourselves.

And our symposium topic — Decoding the Future: Tech Trends and the 21st Century Khilāfah — challenges us to think as builders, as stewards, and as Muslims who understand that we will be held accountable for what we do, and what we fail to do, in this digital age.

PAUSE

We have with us today five remarkable speakers — thinkers, builders, and practitioners — who will each bring a piece of that larger picture into focus.

I am honoured to be your host for this session. Let us begin."

'Building Wealth in the New Age of Technology.'"
